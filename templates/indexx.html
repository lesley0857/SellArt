    My Story

    Birds On a String

    Many believe (me included) that the appearance of a particular bird carries a specific message. For me the sight of Two Magpies means good luck and a sign I'm on the right path. Whereas Robins are a loved one visiting. No matter what you believe I hope you enjoy my little birdies on a string! I really wanted to keep the design delicate allowing for the use of neutral space to represent endless freedom!
